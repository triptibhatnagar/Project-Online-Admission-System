About Us

Maharishi Markandeshwar University Maharishi Markandeshwar University (MMU Mullana or MMDU) is a Deemed-to-be-University that was established in 1993123. It is located in Mullana near Ambala in the state of Haryana, India2. The university has been approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by the NAAC with Grade ‘A’1. It offers undergraduate and post-graduate programs in fields of engineering, hospitality, management, nursing and healthcare2. The university also has two other campuses that are located in Karnal and Solan1.

FAQs

Q. What are the admission requirements in Bachelor's Degree ?

A. Passed 10+2 exam having English as a subject and a minimum of 50% marks (45% in case of SC/ST category) in mandatory subjects.


Q. What is the attendance criteria ?

A. Minimum 90% attendance is compulsory.


Q. What are the transportation options ?

A. Transportation facilities of bus from the nearby districts and areas are available. Free bus service from Mulana bus stand to university is also available.


Q. What financial aids, scholarships, or grants are available ?

A. Maharishi Markandeshwar University (MMU), Mullana offers a variety of scholarships, including:

  • COVID-19 Warriors Scholarship: A 15% tuition waiver for wards of COVID-19 warriors
  • Girl Child Scholarship: A 15% tuition waiver for eligible students with Haryana domicile
  • MMIS Mullana Alumni Scholarship: A 15% tuition waiver for eligible students
  • Merit Scholarship: A scholarship for candidates who meet specific criteria
Students can only receive one MMU scholarship at a time, which includes scholarships, awards, fee waivers, and discounts.


Q. What are the opportunities for extracurricular activities?

A. There are many clubs (NCC, NSS, AARYA, GDSC), startup communities and sports team(Cricket, Tug of War, Basketball) you can join. There are opportunities for for leadership, volunteer work and internships.
